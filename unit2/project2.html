What makes a good network administrator?

The Network Administrator's role is to ensure the stable operation of the computer networks.

    This includes:
  • planning
  • developing
  • installing
  • configuring
  • maintaining
  • supporting
  • and optimizing all:
    • network
    • hardware
    • communication links
